Internal Loan Approval Policy – Retail Banking

1. Eligibility Criteria
- Applicant must be a resident Indian citizen
- Minimum age: 21 years
- Maximum age at loan maturity: 60 years
- Minimum monthly income: INR 25,000

2. Documentation
Mandatory documents include:
- PAN Card
- Aadhaar Card
- Income proof (salary slips or bank statements)
- Address proof

3. Credit Risk Assessment
- Credit score must be above 650
- Applicants with prior loan defaults require manual review

4. Approval Rules
- Applications meeting all criteria may be auto-approved
- Applications with missing documents or low credit score must be sent for manual review
- Applications with fraudulent indicators must be rejected

5. Compliance
All approvals must comply with RBI KYC and AML guidelines.

Retail Credit Risk Team
