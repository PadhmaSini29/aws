Medical Insurance Information

The company provides comprehensive medical insurance coverage to all full-time employees through Allstate Insurance Company.

Insurance Provider:
Allstate Insurance Company

Coverage Details:
- Employee medical coverage is included by default.
- Optional coverage is available for spouse and dependent children.
- Coverage begins from the employee’s date of joining.

Key Benefits:
- Inpatient and outpatient hospital coverage
- Emergency medical services
- Prescription drug coverage
- Preventive health checkups (once per year)

Customer Support:
Allstate Insurance Company provides 24/7 customer support for all insurance-related queries.

Customer Support Phone Number:
+1-800-255-7828

Claims Process:
Employees can file insurance claims through the Allstate Insurance online portal or by contacting customer support.
Required documents include hospital bills, discharge summary, and claim form.

Exclusions:
- Cosmetic procedures
- Non-prescribed treatments
- Pre-existing conditions during the initial waiting period

For further assistance, employees may contact the HR department or reach out directly to Allstate Insurance Company.
